    Courses

    Course Listings

    • Introduction to Computer Science

      Introduction to the principles of computer science, including algorithm design and data structures. This course is suitable for students with little or no programming experience.

      Prerequisites: None

    • Data Structures and Algorithms

      An in-depth study of data structures and algorithms, with an emphasis on their application to real-world problems. This course is suitable for students with a solid background in programming.

      Prerequisites: Introduction to Computer Science

    • Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

      An in-depth study of Artificial Intelligence , with an emphasis on their application to real-world problems. This course is suitable for students with a solid background in programming.

      Prerequisites: Introduction to Artificial intelligence

    • Introduction to machine learning

      An in-depth study of Machine Learning, with an emphasis on their application to real-world problems. This course is suitable for students with a solid background in programming.

      Prerequisites: Introduction to Machine learning
